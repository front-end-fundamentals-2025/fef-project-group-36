Nocco Blue Raspberry

NOCCO Blue Raspberry is a carbonated drink enriched with BCAAs (branched-chain amino acids), caffeine, and vitamins. Contains sweeteners. NOCCO has a high caffeine content (55 mg/100 ml). Not recommended for children, pregnant or breastfeeding women, or individuals sensitive to caffeine.
